Shipping Policy

Standard delivery usually takes 3 to 5 business days after fulfillment.
Premium customers should receive proactive follow-up if a delivery promise is
missed.

If a package is delayed, agents should check tracking, confirm the address, and
explain that the carrier status is being reviewed.

If tracking has not updated for 72 hours, the agent should open a carrier
investigation and set a follow-up reminder.

Agents should not promise same-day replacement unless support lead approval is
already available.
