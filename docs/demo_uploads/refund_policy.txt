Refund Policy

Refunds for missing deliveries require order verification, carrier tracking review,
and support lead approval before any refund is promised to the customer.

If tracking has not moved for more than 72 hours, agents should apologize,
confirm the shipping address, and open a carrier investigation.

Refunds for damaged high-value items require photos of the item, packaging
condition, order number, and support lead approval.

Agents must not say that a refund has been processed unless an approval decision
has already been recorded.
