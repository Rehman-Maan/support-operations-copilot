Damaged Item Playbook

When a customer reports a damaged item, agents should ask for clear photos of
the damaged product, photos of the packaging, and the order number.

For low-value items, agents may offer replacement review after collecting
evidence. For high-value items, replacement or refund decisions require support
lead approval.

The reply should acknowledge the inconvenience, avoid blaming the customer or
carrier, and explain the next step clearly.

Agents must not confirm a refund, replacement, or credit until approval is
recorded.
